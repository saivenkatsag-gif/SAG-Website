About RPTO

About Remote Pilot Training Organization

"Empowering Andhra Pradesh with its First Home-Grown DGCA-Approved RPTO."

Established by an Andhra-based company, Samtulana Antarjala Gathaka Pvt Ltd is a DGCA-approved Remote Pilot Training Organization delivering professional, compliant, and industry-ready drone pilot training.

We offer structured training programs that integrate DGCA-mandated theoretical instruction, simulator-based learning, and extensive practical flight training. Our courses prepare pilots for applications such as agriculture drone operations, mapping & surveying, industrial inspection, public safety, and commercial UAV services.

With state-of-the-art infrastructure, certified instructors, DGCA-compliant training drones, and dedicated flying zones, SAMTULANA ANTARJALA GATHAKA PVT LTD provides a professional and safe learning environment aligned with global best practices.

By establishing Andhra's first locally owned RPTO, we take pride in contributing to the state's technological self-reliance and playing a key role in shaping the next generation of skilled drone professionals from Andhra Pradesh for India and beyond.

FAQ's

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services, processes, and what sets us apart.

What types of drones do you offer?+
We offer DGCA-certified agricultural drones including multi-rotor sprayers, mapping drones, and custom payload drones suited for different crop types and farm sizes.
Who can enroll in RPTO training?+
Anyone above 18 years with a Class 10 pass certificate can enroll. Both fresher candidates and experienced pilots looking for DGCA certification are welcome.
Is certification mandatory to operate a drone?+
Yes. As per DGCA regulations, a Remote Pilot Certificate (RPC) is mandatory for operating medium and large category drones commercially in India.
How can I enroll in the pilot training program?+
You can contact us via phone at +91 707080 7749 or email [email protected]. You can also fill in the enquiry form on this page.
Will I get hands-on flying experience?+
Absolutely. Our RPTO program is designed with heavy emphasis on practical flying hours, field operations, and real agri-drone deployment scenarios.
What courses are offered under RPTO?+
We currently offer Small Class Rotorcraft (up to 25 kg) and Medium Class Rotorcraft (up to 50 kg) Remote Pilot Training programs, both 5-day DGCA-compliant courses.
What is the duration of the training program?+
The standard training program runs for 5 days: 3 days theory and 2 days practical, followed by DGCA examination preparation and certification.
What topics are covered in RPTO training?+
Topics include DGCA regulations, air traffic rules, drone maintenance, navigation, emergency procedures, agri-spraying techniques, mapping, and field safety protocols.
